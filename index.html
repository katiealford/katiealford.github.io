aboutKATIEALFORD

Hi! I'm Katie, a Silicon Valley-bred, Oakland-based graphic designer and painter. Living amidst my own organized chaos has lent itself to my passion for precision and keen eye for design. An environmentalist at heart, I grew up acutely aware of both my surroundings and the people that inhabited those spaces, developing into an interest in the human psyche. I was rewarded, in turn, with the ability to consider different perspectives, which manifested itself in ever-evolving, more susceptible art. Wanting to apply this passion and understanding, I began creating meaningful graphics and writing soulful copy for an environmental non-profit, and thus began my love for design with a purpose.
As design has evolved, so have I. Learning front-end web development to further build on my skills has been a difficult but rewarding journey thus far. I love the idea of using the web's full potential to complete challenges, whether for myself or others. When I'm not designing, I can be spotted rock climbing, getting my namaste on, being your dog's new best friend, devouring Tina Fey's Bossy Pants and Sophia Amoruso's #GIRLBOSS for the 'nth time, or perfecting the art of pour-over coffee.
